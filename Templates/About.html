What does this Website do?

This website helps travelers who like to travel in their own car to determine the distance between different governorates. Also, it calculates how much fuel will be consumed in this trip and how much money will the traveler pay for fuel. It also calculates the estimated time to reach the desired governorate

How is the distance calculated?

This website stores the lattitude(x) and longitude(y) of each governorate in a database but because earth is a round sphere and we want to calculate the distance between two positions on it, Pythagoras theorem formula won't work here.Instead we use a formula called the Great Cirlce Distance formula.

What is the Great Cirlce Distance formula?

This formula calculates the distance between two positions on a rounded sphere,It's also called the Haversine formula

What is the mathematical equation for the formula?

a=sin2(Δφ/2) + cos φ1 ⋅ cos φ2 ⋅ sin2(Δλ/2)

c= 2 ⋅ atan2( √a, √(1−a) )

d=R ⋅ c

Where:

φ is latitude

λ is longitude

R is earth’s radius (6,371km)

φ is latitude
